County Commission + Chill
Wednesday, September ??, 5:00pm

Join Asheville DSA, Asheville Food and Beverage United, Asheville For All, and Buncombe Decides for the Buncombe County Commission Meeting, where we’ll present the results of our spring campaign to the commissioners, and tell our housing stories!

This will be a celebration. We’re returning to the county, where we visited last December, to show them that the community is behind the commissioners’ own pursuits in directing the Buncombe County Tourism Development Authority (BCTDA) to help out with the housing crisis.

After the public comment period is over, we’ll continue the celebration at a nearby downtown watering hole, to be announced.

RSVP below to let us know you’re coming! We’ll be sure to keep you up to date on all of the details.

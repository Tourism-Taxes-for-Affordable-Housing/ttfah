What’s the Campaign?

We are asking the Buncombe County Tourism Development Authority (BCTDA) to help out with the affordable housing crisis in Asheville by using its “LIFT Fund” to subsidize homes for working people.

Find and sign our petition here.

You can read more about the “LIFT Fund” and why the BCTDA should send this money to the needs of working people here.
